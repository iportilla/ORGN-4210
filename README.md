# ORGN 4210 · Leeds School of Business

# Thinking with Artificial Intelligence

**Systems Thinking + Modern AI + Real Business Outcomes**

---

> Hands-on. Project-based. · No traditional exams. · No textbook required. · AI tools actively encouraged.

---

## About This Course

Learn to think and act systematically in a world where human and artificial intelligence work side by side. From generative models to autonomous agents, you'll master AI tools to analyze, design, and execute real business solutions — and graduate with a working **one-person venture powered by AI**.

---

## What You'll Explore

- Systems Thinking
- Modern AI Capabilities
- Problem Decomposition
- AI Workflow & Automation
- Decision-Making with AI
- Business Models
- AI Agents & RAG
- Ethics & Governance
- Venture Design

## What You'll Be Able to Do

- → Use AI for analysis, planning & automation
- → Decompose complex business challenges
- → Build AI-enabled workflows
- → Identify leverage points within systems
- → Present AI-driven solutions to stakeholders

---

## Capstone: Your One-Person AI Venture

The course culminates in a capstone project where each student designs and launches their own AI-powered venture — demonstrating real business value from day one.
